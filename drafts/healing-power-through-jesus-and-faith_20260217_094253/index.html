Holy Spirit & Power

Healing Power Through Jesus and Faith

At a Glance

Big Idea
The Bible presents Jesus as the One anointed to heal and deliver, and it also shows that Gods power can be present to heal in real moments of need. From desperate cries to simple acts of reaching out, Scripture highlights faith that connects to Christs power. This study traces how Gods Word produces faith, how believers respond, and what healed lives look like afterward.
Primary Passages
Acts 10:38 Psalms 107:17-20 Luke 5:17 Mark 5:25-34 Matthew 15:21-28 John 14:12

Key Takeaways

  • Jesus was anointed by God to heal and to deliver people from oppression (Acts 10:38).
  • God sent His word to heal and rescue, showing the Words central role in healing faith (Psalms 107:17-20; John 1:1-3).
  • There are moments when Gods power is present to heal, and faith responds to that present reality (Luke 5:17; Luke 6:19).
  • Faith can look like persistent crying out, humble dependence, and reaching for Jesusyet it is always Jesus power that heals (Matthew 15:21-28; Mark 5:25-34).
  • Receiving healing calls for obedient action and visible change, as seen in those who rose, walked, and followed Gods work (Acts 3:1-8; Mark 10:46-52).
  • Spiritual growth matters: believers are called to desire the pure milk of the Word so their faith matures (1 Peter 2:2).
